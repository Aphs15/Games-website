Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 2024

RedPixel Gaming respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal information you may provide through our Website. This Privacy Policy describes how we may collect data when you visit our website or make a purchase with us..

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information from you when you:

  • Create an account
  • Purchase a product
  • Sign up for newsletters or updates
  • Contact us for support

The types of personal information we may collect include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Billing and shipping address
  • Payment information (handled securely through third-party payment providers)
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Purchase history and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Process your orders and deliver products to you
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send you updates on new products or offers (only if you opt-in)
  • Maintain website security and prevent fraud

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted third-party service providers, including:

  • Payment processors (to handle transactions)
  • Shipping companies (to deliver products)
  • Analytics providers (to help us improve our website)

These third parties only have access to your information as necessary to perform their services, and they are obligated to protect your information.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience and gather information about how our website is used. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but doing so may affect the functionality of our website.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and security measures to protect your personal information. While we strive to protect your data, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Restrict certain data processing activities

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email provided below.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Please review this policy periodically to stay informed of any changes.
